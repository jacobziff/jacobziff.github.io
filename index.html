About Me

Hello! I am a Computer Science Student in the University of Michigan's class of 2025. In particular, I enjoy working with software. Although I do not have a niche beyond that, I look forward to exploring the broad field of Computer Science! I aim to establish a solid foundation with the courses and activities that I am currently doing so that I can be the best version of myself down the road.

My strengths are my curiosity, consistency, and organization. I am always trying to learn new things, and I love asking questions. My satisfaction comes from not just knowing enough to pass an exam, but having in-depth knowledge of a subject that can allow me to grow as a person. My strategy is to always ensure that I am always putting in effort. I believe that consistent effort adds up over time, and that success is not built overnight. I plan my actions far in advance and keeping myself organized and putting in effort leads me to my successes.
